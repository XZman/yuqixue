About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in ECE at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. I am advised by Prof. Jian Huang as a member of the Systems Platform Research Group. My research interest is AI infrastructure, with a focus on system and architectural techniques for low-cost AI.

Prior to my Ph.D. study, I obtained my Bachelor's degree from UIUC in 2021.
